🏆 Winner: GIF Creative
Product GIFs (room context) score 94/100 — highest of all formats. They combine motion (attention) with product clarity (conversion intent) at lower production cost than video. Scaling GIF allocation from 2% → 13.1% generated $369K/month incremental revenue.

⚠️ The Engagement Paradox

Key Finding: High engagement ≠ High revenue. UGC testimonials generate 12.4% engagement (likes, comments, shares) but only 1.9x ROAS. Meanwhile, Product GIFs get 2.3% engagement but deliver 9.2x ROAS.

Why: Engagement measures entertainment value. ROAS measures purchase intent. People love watching testimonials, but they don't buy from them. Product GIFs show the item clearly — less entertaining, more converting.
